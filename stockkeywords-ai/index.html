Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best keyword tool for Adobe Stock contributors?

StockKeywords AI is a good choice. It suggests titles and generates 49 keywords in ~10 seconds using your own API key, organizes them into categories, and fills the upload form. The current launch build is free.

How do I auto-generate keywords for Adobe Stock photos?

Install StockKeywords AI, add your API key in options, open Adobe Stock contributor upload page, click "Generate Keywords", then "Fill All". The whole process takes about 10 seconds.

Is there a free AI keyword generator for stock photos?

Yes. The current launch build is free, and you bring your own API key. Gemini has a free tier, and you can also point OpenAI-compatible requests at supported relay or cloud gateway URLs.

How to save time tagging Adobe Stock images?

Use StockKeywords AI to auto-generate 49 keywords instead of typing them manually. The extension organizes keywords and fills the form with one click.

Does StockKeywords AI work on Microsoft Edge?

Yes. StockKeywords AI is built and tested for both Chrome and Edge from Day 1. The launch build is free on both browsers.

Can I use my own API key?

Yes. That's the whole point! You bring your own OpenAI or Gemini API key. You control your API usage and costs directly with the provider.

What data does StockKeywords AI collect?

None. StockKeywords AI runs in your browser and does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data. The only network requests are direct requests from your browser to the AI endpoint you configure and optional license validation logic reserved for a later paid rollout. See our privacy policy.
