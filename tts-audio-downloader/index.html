TTS Audio Downloader

Download text-to-speech audio as MP3 or WAV from Google Cloud TTS, Azure Speech Studio, and ChatGPT. No ads. No server upload. One click.

Install for Chrome Install for Edge

TTS Audio Downloader is a Chrome and Edge extension that adds a download button to text-to-speech demo pages. Open the Google Cloud TTS demo, Azure Speech Studio, or ChatGPT, type your text and play it, then click the download button to save the audio as MP3 or WAV — instantly, with no server upload and no sign-up required.

The basic plan includes 5,000 characters per day with no per-request limit, MP3 and WAV export, and unlimited ChatGPT Download All. Pro ($0.99/month or $4.99 one-time) unlocks unlimited characters, bulk mode, custom file naming, and AAC export. The extension never shows ads or pop-up promotions.

All audio processing happens locally in your browser. Your text is never sent to our servers. The extension only accesses three specific TTS platforms — no broad permissions required.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download audio from Google TTS?

Install TTS Audio Downloader, open the Google Cloud TTS demo page, type your text and click Play. A Download MP3 button appears next to the play button. Click it to save the audio file instantly. The extension handles long text automatically by splitting, synthesizing, and merging into one file.

Can I download ChatGPT voice messages?

Yes. TTS Audio Downloader adds a download button next to each ChatGPT voice response. You can download individual messages or use Download All to save every voice message in a conversation. Download All is included in the basic plan at no cost.

What audio formats are supported?

The basic plan supports MP3 and WAV export. Pro users additionally get AAC export for ChatGPT (native quality, smaller file size). You can switch formats with one click in the popup or on the download button.

Is TTS Audio Downloader available at no cost?

The basic plan includes 5,000 characters per day, MP3 and WAV export, and unlimited ChatGPT Download All. No sign-up or credit card required. Pro ($0.99/month or $4.99 one-time) unlocks unlimited characters, bulk mode, custom file naming, and AAC export.

Does the extension collect my text or audio data?

No. All text processing and audio conversion happens entirely within your browser. Your text is never sent to our servers. Audio is downloaded directly from the TTS platform to your device. The extension does not include any analytics or tracking code.

Does it work on Microsoft Edge?

Yes. TTS Audio Downloader is available on both Chrome Web Store and Microsoft Edge Add-ons. The features are identical on both browsers.
