Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download audio from Google TTS?

Install TTS Audio Downloader, open the Google Cloud TTS demo page, type your text and click Play. The audio file downloads automatically — no extra button to click. The extension handles long text automatically by splitting, synthesizing, and merging into one file.

Can I download ChatGPT voice messages?

Yes. TTS Audio Downloader adds a download button next to each ChatGPT voice response. You can download individual messages or use Download All to save every voice message in a conversation. It also includes a TTS input box for generating speech from any text.

What audio formats are supported?

MP3 and WAV. You can switch formats with one click in the popup.

Is TTS Audio Downloader available at no cost?

The basic plan includes 5,000 characters per day, MP3 and WAV export, and unlimited ChatGPT Download All. No sign-up or credit card required. Pro ($1.99 one-time) unlocks unlimited downloads forever.

Does the extension collect my text or audio data?

No. All text processing and audio conversion happens entirely within your browser. Your text is never sent to our servers. Audio is downloaded directly from the TTS platform to your device.

Does it work on Microsoft Edge?

Yes. TTS Audio Downloader is available on both Chrome Web Store and Microsoft Edge Add-ons. The features are identical on both browsers.
