Features

Bilingual Subtitles

See English and your native language side-by-side. Subtitles are intercepted directly from YouTube's API — no third-party translation service delays.

Click-to-Lookup

Click any word in the subtitles to instantly see its pronunciation, part of speech, definitions, and example sentences. Audio pronunciation included.

Vocabulary Book

Saved words are organized with full context: the sentence where you found the word, the video timestamp, phonetics, and multiple definitions.

Anki Export (Pro)

Export your vocabulary as .apkg files ready for Anki spaced repetition. Each card includes word, pronunciation, definition, context sentence, and video link.

Sentence Loop (Pro)

Loop any subtitle sentence for focused listening practice. Perfect for shadowing exercises and catching difficult phrases.

Flashcard Study

Built-in flashcard mode with flip-to-reveal. Study your saved vocabulary with audio pronunciation without leaving the browser.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which languages are supported for translation?

LangTube translates English subtitles into 14+ languages including Chinese (Simplified/Traditional), Japanese, Korean, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Russian, Arabic, Hindi, Thai, Vietnamese, and Indonesian.

Does it work on all YouTube videos?

LangTube works on any YouTube video that has English subtitles (auto-generated or manual). If a video has no English subtitle track, LangTube will show a friendly message.

How is click-to-lookup powered?

LangTube uses the Free Dictionary API and Wiktionary as fallback sources. Definitions include IPA pronunciation, multiple parts of speech, example sentences, and audio pronunciation.

What data does LangTube collect?

LangTube processes everything locally in your browser. Subtitle data, vocabulary, and dictionary lookups are stored on your device. The only network requests are for translation (Google Translate API) and license verification for Pro users.

How is this different from Language Reactor?

LangTube focuses on active vocabulary building rather than passive subtitle reading. One-click word saving, built-in flashcards, Anki export, and a dedicated vocabulary book make LangTube the better choice for serious learners — at a fraction of the price.
