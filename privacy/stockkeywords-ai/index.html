Privacy Policy

StockKeywords AI — Last updated: April 24, 2026

StockKeywords AI is designed to run locally in your browser. It helps Adobe Stock contributors generate title suggestions and keywords using their own AI API credentials.

What we store

  • Your selected provider, model, and prompt settings.
  • Your API key and optional base URL in browser extension storage.
  • Local usage counters and feature preferences.
  • Optional license key information if paid mode is enabled in a future release.

What we do not collect

  • We do not collect your browsing history.
  • We do not collect Adobe Stock account credentials.
  • We do not transmit your Adobe Stock form data to ExtPilot servers.
  • We do not sell or rent your data.

How the extension works

The extension reads visible Adobe Stock page context in your browser so it can generate metadata and fill the title and keyword fields. AI requests are sent directly from the extension using the provider configuration you enter.

Third-party services

  • OpenAI-compatible APIs / Gemini: Metadata generation requests go to the provider endpoint you configure.
  • License validation: Paid-mode validation logic may contact ExtPilot license infrastructure in a future paid rollout. The current launch build keeps this capability reserved but pricing is inactive.

Data retention

Extension settings remain in browser storage until you remove the extension or clear extension data. You control your own API provider account and any data retained by that provider under its own policies.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact support@extpilot.com.
