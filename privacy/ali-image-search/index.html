Privacy Policy

AliFind — Image Search for AliExpress — Last updated: April 11, 2026

Overview

AliFind is a free browser extension that lets you search for products on AliExpress by right-clicking images or entering keywords. It is designed with privacy as a priority: we collect no personal data, use no analytics, and store nothing on our servers.

Data We Collect

None. AliFind does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no accounts, no login, and no registration.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your browsing history or browsing activity.
  • We do not collect or store the images you search.
  • We do not collect your search queries or keywords.
  • We do not use any analytics or tracking services (no Google Analytics, no telemetry).
  • We do not collect any personally identifiable information.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties.

Data Storage

All extension settings are stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.local. This data never leaves your browser. You can clear it at any time by removing the extension.

Network Requests

When you perform an image search, AliFind sends the image URL to our search proxy server to find matching products. This request contains only the image URL — no cookies, no user identifiers, and no personal data are included.

When you perform a keyword search, AliFind opens AliExpress search results directly in your browser. No data passes through our servers.

Affiliate Links

Product links in search results may contain affiliate parameters via the Admitad network. If you make a purchase through these links, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. No personal data is shared with Admitad — affiliate tracking is handled entirely by AliExpress cookies after you click a link.

Permissions

The extension requests only the permissions necessary for its core functionality:

  • contextMenus: To add the "Search this image on AliExpress" option to the right-click menu.
  • storage: To save your extension preferences locally on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
