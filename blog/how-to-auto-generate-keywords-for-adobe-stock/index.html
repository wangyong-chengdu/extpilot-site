How to Auto-Generate Keywords for Adobe Stock in 10 Seconds

Published April 24, 2026 · 5 min read

TL;DR: Open the Adobe Stock contributor uploads page, click Suggest Titles or Generate Keywords, then use Use Title or Fill All. StockKeywords AI uses your own Gemini or OpenAI-compatible API key and keeps the workflow inside Adobe Stock.

Why this workflow matters

Adobe Stock metadata work is repetitive. You need a strong title, you need relevant keywords, and you need both of them inside the same upload form. Most contributors lose time copying text between AI tools, notes, and the Adobe editor.

StockKeywords AI collapses that workflow into one place. The extension sits on the contributor uploads page, reads the current asset context, and gives you title suggestions and keywords without leaving Adobe Stock.

Step 1: Add your API settings

  1. Open the extension options page.
  2. Select Gemini or an OpenAI-compatible provider.
  3. Paste your API key.
  4. Optionally set a custom base URL if you use a relay, gateway, or cloud vendor endpoint.
  5. Save and test the connection.

Step 2: Open the Adobe Stock edit screen

Go to the contributor uploads page and open an asset in the editing view where you can see the title and keyword fields. That is the screen where the extension injects its control panel.

Step 3: Ask AI for titles or keywords

You have two separate actions:

  • Suggest Titles runs a dedicated title prompt and returns several title options.
  • Generate Keywords returns 49 keywords grouped into Caption, Top, Next, and Other.

This separation matters because title work and keyword work have different quality constraints. You can accept one suggested title without affecting the keyword generation flow.

Step 4: Fill the Adobe Stock form

When you like a title, click Use Title. When you like the keyword result, click Fill All. The extension fills Adobe Stock’s native fields instead of dumping text into a separate overlay.

FAQ

Do I need to use the official OpenAI endpoint?

No. The extension supports custom base URLs for compatible services.

Does it overwrite my title automatically?

No. Title suggestions are opt-in. You choose a suggestion with Use Title.
