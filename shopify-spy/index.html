Frequently Asked Questions

Does this work with any Shopify store?

Yes. It works with any store built on Shopify, including those using custom domains. The extension detects Shopify stores automatically.

Is this legal?

The extension accesses publicly available product data through Shopify's public products.json endpoint, which is accessible to any browser visitor.

What data is collected?

None. All processing happens locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any scraped data or browsing activity.

Can I scrape stores with thousands of products?

Yes. Pro mode handles large catalogs with pagination. The export may take a bit longer for very large stores.
