Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to WhatsApp Bulk Sender & Privacy Guard by ExtPilot.

Data Processing

The extension runs on WhatsApp Web and processes data locally inside your browser. Message content, contact lists, and send history are handled on-device for product functionality.

Permissions

The extension requests access to `*://*.whatsapp.com/*` so it can work on WhatsApp Web. It also uses browser storage for settings, templates, privacy preferences, and send history.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not upload your chats or contact lists to our servers as part of the core sending flow.
  • We do not request broad `all_urls` host access.
  • We do not require account sign-up to use the free version.

Local Storage

The extension may store settings, templates, license state, and send history in browser local storage to support pause/resume, export, and UI persistence.

Payments

If you upgrade to Pro in the future, payment handling will be processed by Stripe. ExtPilot does not store raw credit card information inside the extension.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to support@extpilot.com.
